What

SOCIA is a platform that simulates a Security Operations Center (SOC), integrating Artificial Intelligence technologies to offer unique training in cybersecurity incident management.

Read documentation

How

SOCIA integrates advanced technologies of Artificial Intelligence with innovative methodologies to create the most realistic learning experience in cybersecurity.

  • Real-time SOC Simulation

    Practical training in a simulated Security Operations Center that replicates real cybersecurity scenarios.

  • AI-powered Tutoring

    AI assistant that adapts its level of help based on your response history, providing a personalized experience.

  • Challenge-Based Learning

    Methodology that allows applying knowledge practically through solving real incidents.

  • Open Source

    Open source platform that allows replication and adaptation in other educational centers and companies.
